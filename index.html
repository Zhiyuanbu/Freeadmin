About the Game

Free Admin is a fun and chaotic Roblox game where everyone gets admin powers! Use commands, prank your friends, and explore endless possibilities. No purchase required—just jump in and enjoy!

Features

  • Unlimited admin commands for everyone
  • Frequent updates and new commands
  • Safe and fun environment for all ages
  • Invite your friends and form teams

Popular Admin Commands

  • :fly – Fly around the map!
  • :kick [player] – Kick a player (for fun!)
  • :sparkles – Add sparkles to yourself
  • :invisible – Become invisible
  • And many more...
